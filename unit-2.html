Key Learnings

Holistic Risk View

ERM moves beyond siloed risk management to provide an enterprise-wide perspective, considering how risks interrelate across the organization.

Business Impact Focus

The emphasis shifted from technical vulnerabilities to business consequences, aligning risk management with organizational objectives.

Framework Flexibility

Understanding that methodologies like FAIR can be adapted conceptually even when full implementation isn't feasible for the project scope.
